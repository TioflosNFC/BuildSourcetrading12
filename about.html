Material supply, run the way a finishing site actually needs it.

Build Source started with a simple frustration: finishing materials in Addis Ababa were scattered across too many suppliers, with too little consistency in stock or delivery. We built the company to fix that.

Our Story

One source for the materials that finish a building.

Gypsum board, ceiling grids, partition framing, soundproofing and waterproofing all come from different specialists by default. Contractors end up coordinating five deliveries instead of one.

Build Source consolidates eight of the most-used finishing material lines under one supplier, one delivery schedule, and one point of contact — so the people doing the finishing work can focus on the finishing work.
